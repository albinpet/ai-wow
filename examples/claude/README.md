## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Open Claude** at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. **Copy prompts** from the example files
3. **Adapt them** to your specific needs
4. **Iterate** based on Claude's responses

## 💡 Best Practices

- **Be specific** in your prompts and provide context
- **Ask follow-up questions** to dive deeper into solutions
- **Request alternatives** when you need different approaches
- **Use Claude for explanation** of complex concepts or code
- **Combine with other tools** like Cursor for maximum efficiency
